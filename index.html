On July 31st, 2015 I was diagnosed with breast cancer.

Good news: I’m going to be fine.
Bad news: It’s going to suck. Probably.

Texting with Cancer is the mental conversation with optimism and pessimism as I deal with cancer and life.

"It's our choice, whether to hate something in our lives or to love every moment of them, even the parts that bring us pain. 'At every moment, we are volunteers.'”
- Joel Lovell on Stephen Colbert

Inspired by Before We Met
With a lot of help from Laith Azzam

Thanks to my family who are making this way, way easier to deal with.
Thanks to my friends who put up with my cancer jokes. (Is cancer contagious?)

And thanks, art + tech community, for making me feel alive.

Love,
Natalie Sun
